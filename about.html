What is Vastu Shastra?

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science of architecture and design that focuses on creating harmony between human beings and their environment. It involves the arrangement and design of buildings, homes, and spaces to ensure they are aligned with natural forces and energies.

The Principles of Vastu

  • Space Orientation: Aligning rooms, doors, and windows based on cardinal directions for positive energy.
  • Balance of Elements: Proper placement of elements like fire, water, earth, and air.
  • Energy Flow: Ensuring the smooth flow of energy through the space for health, prosperity, and happiness.

Why Choose Vastu?

Vastu Shastra can help improve the well-being of individuals, reduce stress, enhance relationships, and create a peaceful environment. Whether you're designing a new home or improving an existing space, Vastu ensures that the energies within your home work in harmony with nature.
