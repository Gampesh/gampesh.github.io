Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use in your packaging? ⌄

We use premium kraft paper, corrugated board, and specialized materials depending on your requirements. All materials are sourced from certified suppliers and meet international standards. We also offer eco-friendly and recycled options for environmentally conscious clients.

Can you provide custom designs? ⌄

Absolutely! We specialize in custom packaging solutions. Our design team can work with your specifications to create packaging that perfectly matches your brand and product requirements. We offer free design consultation and samples.

What is your minimum order quantity? ⌄

Our minimum order quantities vary depending on the product type and customization level. For standard products, we can work with smaller orders. For custom designs, we typically require minimum quantities. Please contact us for specific details on your project.

How long does it take to deliver? ⌄

Delivery times depend on the order size and complexity. Standard products typically ship within 5-7 business days. Custom orders may take 2-3 weeks depending on design and production requirements. We offer expedited services for urgent orders.

Do you serve small businesses? ⌄

Yes! We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to large corporations. We understand the unique needs of small businesses and offer flexible solutions with competitive pricing. Our team is happy to help you find cost-effective packaging solutions.
